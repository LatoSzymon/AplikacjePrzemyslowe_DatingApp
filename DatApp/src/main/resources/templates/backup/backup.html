About Profile Backup

The backup feature allows you to:

  • Export: Download all your profile data in a portable format (JSON or XML)
  • Import: Restore your profile from a previously saved backup file
  • Migrate: Transfer your data between different accounts or devices
  • Archive: Keep a personal copy of your dating history for your records
Privacy Note: Your backup files contain personal information. Store them securely and never share them with anyone.
